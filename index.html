Who I Am

Nanthavath Senthavy

Graphic Designer & Video Editor

I'm Nanthavath — also known as Do. A graphic designer and video editor with a passion for bringing ideas to life through motion, visuals, and storytelling.

Born in 1995 in Vientiane, Laos, I've spent the past nine years building expertise across graphic design, motion graphics, video editing, and marketing communications — working with international hotel brands and agencies across the region.

From Facebook ad campaigns and brand identity to After Effects animations and Premiere Pro edits — I create work that communicates clearly, moves people, and looks good doing it.

I also integrate AI into my creative process — using Gemini, Claude, Google Flow, and custom AI agents to accelerate ideation, automate repetitive tasks, and push what's possible as a one-person creative.
